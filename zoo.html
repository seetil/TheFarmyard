Garden Center

Our extensive garden center based at the carpark entrance is full of thriving plants, flowers, soils, gardening tools, garden furniture and much more. Our friendly staff can assist you with finding the perfect plant for you-- just ask!


Nature Walks

We are home to a wonderful selection of nature trails and adventures to keep the family active and fit. Our trails range from short walks around the loch- where you can feed the ducks and visit our huge kids play park on the way, to longer more adventurous treks around the hills- where the scenery is unbeateable!
